About Me

I am a software developer living in Austin, Texas. I found my passion for tech while studying for my bachelor’s degree at California State University, Fullerton. As a Business Administration major, an introduction to Information Systems was part of our curriculum. It quickly became my favorite class and led to my decision to choose this discipline as my concentration.


While I worked as a technical consultant, it was necessary for me to comprehend the requirements of the end user and be able to effectively translate them to our development team. To understand the technical side better, I decided to teach myself programming. This quickly evolved into a passion and I knew that I wanted to pursue it as my career. Over the past year, I have taught myself the fundamentals of web development using today's popular technologies including Ruby on Rails, React, Redux, PostgreSQL, and more - take a look at my projects. I am seeking an established company that will launch my career as a software developer.
